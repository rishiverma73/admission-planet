Pharmacy Programs

D.Pharm (Diploma in Pharmacy)

  • Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Biology/Mathematics, Minimum 50% marks
  • Course Content: Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Hospital Pharmacy
  • Career Opportunities:
    • Community Pharmacist
    • Drug Inspector
    • Medical Representative
    • Pharmacy Technician
  • Average Starting Salary: ₹1.8 - ₹3.5 LPA

B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy)

  • Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB/PCM, Minimum 50% marks
  • Course Content: Medicinal Chemistry, Pharmacognosy, Drug Delivery Systems, Clinical Pharmacy
  • Career Opportunities:
    • Clinical Pharmacist
    • Pharmaceutical Researcher
    • Quality Control Analyst
    • Regulatory Affairs Officer
  • Average Starting Salary: ₹3 - ₹6 LPA

M.Pharm (Master of Pharmacy)

  • Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)
  • Eligibility: B.Pharm with minimum 55% marks + GPAT score
  • Specializations:
    • Pharmaceutics (Drug Formulation)
    • Pharmacology (Drug Action)
    • Pharmacy Practice (Clinical)
    • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Average Starting Salary: ₹4 - ₹8 LPA

Pharm.D (Doctor of Pharmacy)

  • Duration: 6 years (5 years academics + 1 year internship)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB, Minimum 50% marks
  • Course Content: Clinical Pharmacy, Pharmacotherapeutics, Hospital Training, Community Pharmacy
  • Career Opportunities:
    • Clinical Pharmacist in Hospitals
    • Hospital Pharmacy Director
    • Pharmaceutical Researcher
    • Drug Policy Maker
  • Average Starting Salary: ₹5 - ₹10 LPA
