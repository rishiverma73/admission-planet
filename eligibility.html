Eligibility Criteria

Check the qualification requirements for various courses

Undergraduate Degree Eligibility

B.A. (Bachelor of Arts)

  • Completion of high school (10+2) from recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (varies by university)
  • Some universities may require entrance exams

B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science)

  • 10+2 with Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics)
  • Minimum 50-60% aggregate marks (subject to university norms)
  • Some courses may have specific subject requirements

B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)

  • 10+2 with Commerce stream preferred
  • Minimum 45-50% aggregate marks
  • Mathematics as a subject may be required for some universities

B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology)

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics
  • Minimum 60-75% aggregate marks (varies by college)
  • Valid score in JEE Main/State Engineering Entrance Exams

Graduate Degree Eligibility

M.A. (Master of Arts)

  • Bachelor's degree (B.A. or equivalent) in relevant discipline
  • Minimum 50-55% aggregate marks
  • Some universities conduct entrance exams

M.Sc. (Master of Science)

  • B.Sc. in relevant science field
  • Minimum 55-60% aggregate marks
  • Entrance exams for some universities and specializations

M.Com (Master of Commerce)

  • B.Com or equivalent degree
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Some universities prefer entrance exam scores

MBA (Master of Business Administration)

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid score in CAT/MAT/XAT/CMAT or university entrance tests
  • Work experience preferred but not mandatory

M.Tech (Master of Technology)

  • B.Tech/B.E. in relevant engineering discipline
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks
  • Valid GATE score or university entrance exam

Diploma & Certificate Programs Eligibility

ANM (Auxiliary Nursing Midwifery)

  • 10+2 with Science subjects preferred
  • Minimum 45-50% aggregate marks
  • Age limit: 17-35 years
  • Some states conduct entrance exams

GNM (General Nursing Midwifery)

  • 10+2 with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Age limit: 17-35 years
  • State nursing council entrance exams may apply

Polytechnic Diploma

  • 10th pass for 3-year diploma
  • 10+2 with Science for lateral entry to 2nd year
  • Minimum 35-50% marks depending on state
  • State-level entrance exams in most cases

BLIS (Bachelor of Library Science)

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 45-50% aggregate marks
  • Some universities conduct entrance exams

B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy)

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (60% for some universities)
  • Valid score in state/national pharmacy entrance exams

LLB/BA LLB (Law Programs)

  • For LLB: Bachelor's degree with 45-50% marks
  • For BA LLB: 10+2 with 45-50% marks
  • Valid score in CLAT/LSAT or university entrance tests
  • Age limit may apply for some programs

Doctoral Programs Eligibility

Ph.D. (Doctor of Philosophy)

  • Master's degree in relevant field with minimum 55% marks
  • Valid score in UGC-NET/JRF or university entrance exam
  • Research proposal submission
  • Interview with the research committee
  • Some universities may require prior research experience
